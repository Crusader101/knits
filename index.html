ABOUT US

We are a home based business located in Pretoria, South Africa. All of our products and accessories are made by hand with care and a lot of love. We are proud of our products and methods.

I knit myself as well as own the company. Other women throughout the local community work with me to provide our products and lend their unique talents for custom requests. We are women from different walks of life, each with their own story and experiences.    

 Our gratititude goes out to all of our customers, your purchases give us a sense of belonging to something larger than our immediate lives.  Allow us to become part of your story, with a special gift, a warm family heirloom, or remembrance of a special occassion.
